OLD MEDIA AND NEW FORM REA!TALK'22

Milan
2022

The event titled "Exploring Novel Avenues of Artistic Expression: Traditional and Emerging Media" was arranged by Rea! in October 2022 at the iconic Milanese venue Fabbrica del Vapore.
Our team, alongside three other distinguished artists, was invited to showcase our respective works and engage in a thought-provoking discourse on the intricate interplay between technology, nature, and design.
We express our heartfelt gratitude to Rea! and all affiliated institutions for affording us this invaluable opportunity to participate in such an enlightening exchange.
