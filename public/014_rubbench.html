RUBBENCH

Barcelona
2022

Public seating bench made from recycled cans and plastic, filled with crushed plastic and concrete, it aims to turn a fun and innnovate look on waste produced by festivals and concerts.
With 144 recovered metal cans, plastic waste, leftover cement and metal construction bars, we built an individual module.
