Abundance

Barcelona
2024

ABUNDANCE is a temporary light installation at Pumerejo. The iconic club in Hospitalet di Llobregat comissioned a set design that would change the perception of the space for one night.

This ephemeral installation explores a series of low-impact ways of transforming a space, with resuable and low-consumption LED lights. The project aims to introduce perfectly geometrical shapes like catenary curves into the space.
