TERRAIN LAB

Sicily
2022

The project aims to comprehend the fishing and soil exploitation patterns of the island, together with its history and growing tourism, to influate its future by creating a series of mappings and policies to be presented to local authorities and influence the way these are built and used.
This action plan showcases our proposal for the currents, fishing routes, immigration routes, marine diversity centers and shore commerce in the Mediterrenean sea.
