ALEGRE

Milan
2024

Alegre - Navigli, Xaarchive's first restaurant project in Milan, is open at Via Watt 32. Located within the iconic Italian recording studio MOYSA, this space reflects the innovative spirit of Alegre.

The design incorporates natural materials such as wood, ceramic, and various vegetation, creating a warm and organic ambiance. This is complemented by modern elements, including Milan's first 3D printed bar front, showcasing the seamless integration of technology and design.

3D printed ceramic, and PETG are the key design factors that elevate the space.
