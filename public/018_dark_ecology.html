DARK ECOLOGY

Buenos Aires
2022

Energy production through photosynthesis. We installed a bronze network in fertile soil and tested how energy is realeased by the plant as a part of its organic process.
The result was in a positive energetic charge that could be stored and potentially light up a single bulb.
