SPORE ARCADE

Eindhoven
2023

Spore Arcade proposes a new take on decomposition and what we view as "waste" Many man-made structures dacay over time, and there's a lack of rationalization of this process:
But what if we saw decay as a potential rather than a barrier?
Spore Arcade aims to change the way we live, inhabit, feed ourselves and age. 3 printed myscellium arches interact with italian ruins in a search for a more cohesive and sustainable future.
