Istante Urbano

Pesaro
2024

Comissioned by Pesaro 2024 - Capitale italiana della cultura, we have been called to re-imagine public art in a conjunct exhibition with iconic Italian artist Arnaldo Pomodoro.

Istante Urbano aims to rethink how we approach public space, what technologies and innovation we put out into the general public, and the beauty numeric data can evoke, in this case regarding the urban density of the city of Pesaro.

We thank Fondazione Arnaldo Pomodoro and the curators that made possible this exhibition: Pippo Ciorra, Cornelia Mattiacci, Michele Giorgi and Carola Nava.

Gifting the city a reflection of itself.
