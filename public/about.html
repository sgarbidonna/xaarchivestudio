XAARCHIVE STUDIO is a Female-led Architectures & Design Studio founded in 2022, based in Milan and Barcelona.
We specialize in interior design and installations, and our work relies in the confines of nature and artificiality. Trained as architects, we believe the inclusion of all forms of life is valid and necessary and we often design with vegetation, organic materials merged with hyper-tech processes and aesthetics.


institutions
The United Nations, Dutch Design Week Eindhoven, IAAC (Insititute of Advanced Architecture Cataluyna) Barcelona, FAO Rome, Next Nature Netherlands, Evoluon Design Museum, Spazio Serra Gallery, Centro Arte Visive Pescheria Pesaro, Fondazione Arnaldo Pomodoro, Rea! Art Fair Milan.


our services include,

ARCHITECTURE & INTERIOR DESIGN
Trained as architects, our team specializes in interior design ranging from private residencies and hospitality spaces to public pavilions with ephemeral or permanent quality.

INSTALLATIONS
We design ephemeral installations like pop-up stores, set designs for venues or photoshoots as well as specific objects, including permanent 3D printed sculptures, light design, and store fronts.

VISUALIZATION
The creation of hyper realistic images and renderings is a key element in our design, it allows our clients to imagine the space with upmost accuracy. Through renders, digital sets & virtual environments we deliver innovative content that empowers brands to amplify their message effectively.

PARAMETRIC DESIGN
Counting with programmers in our team, we differentiate ourselves by taking a hyper-tech approach to design immersed in new technologies like 3D printing and code-based 3D geometry that allows us to create unique and efficient spaces & projects. We can develop projects based on client-provided data and are able to manage large datasets to improve technical qualities or aesthetic needs of each project.

Several works have been published in magazines like La Repubblica, La Stampa, Corriere della Sera, BUENA, AY Mag, Chiasmo, amongst others.
