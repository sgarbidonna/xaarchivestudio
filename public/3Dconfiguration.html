XAARCHIVE's personalized 3D configurator

Parametric Modeling

We empower businesses selling variable products to offer unparalleled customization and efficiency. Our parametric models allow for dynamic adjustments to dimensions, shapes, and features, enabling clients to easily tailor their products to customer specifications without starting from scratch each time. This means faster design iterations, precise customization, and a consistent quality across all product variations.

By leveraging our expertise, companies can enhance their product offerings, reduce maual design costs, and deliver a superior, personalized experience, ultimately driving increased customer satisfaction and higher sales.

Export to PDF, DWG or others

If desired, the DWG or 3D model iterations can be downloaded too, and added to on-going 3D plans and architecture renderings.
