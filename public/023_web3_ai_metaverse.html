WEB3, AI, METAVERSE

Torino
2023

We had the honor of participating in Torino Digital Days, where we were invited to be part of a distinguished talk titled "Web3, AI, Metaverse: The Art of Sustainable Innovation."
Alongside fellow entrepreneurs from the new technologies sector, we engaged in a thoughtful conversation, delving into our perspectives on the appropriate utilization and advancement of new technologies within the framework of our traditional practices.
The event, held in Torino, Italy, provided an enriching atmosphere, immersed in the rich heritage, culture, and context of Italy, resulting in a captivating and intellectually stimulating discussion.
