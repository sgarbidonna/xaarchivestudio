ÓSMOSIS

Buenos Aires
2019

Architecture and urban site intervention competition surrounding the University Torcuato Di Tella in Buenos Aires. This project merges computational design with a practical environment and site-managing.

It won 3rd place in the competition and was the only student-selected project among professors’ and recent grad projects.
