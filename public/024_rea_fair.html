REA FAIR

Milan
2022

In 2022, we actively took part in the prestigious Milanese art-fair Rea, where our artwork titled HONGUERA was showcased. Our participation involved exhibiting at the renowned Italian venue known as Fabbica del vapore, located in Milan, alongside more than 100 artists hailing from various countries.

It is worth noting that our sculpture garnered significant recognition, as it was awarded with the first-place honor among all the competing artworks, earning us the esteemed N1 Cash Prize, which was selected by the Artsted team.
