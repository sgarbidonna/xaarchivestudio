Bloom

Lecce
2024

Bloom: Biosfera Urbana works like a portable lung, a movable space to be placed in the central most dense areas of a city, inviting visitors to enter and take a moment for themselves.

Its dense vegetation serves as a sound barrier and a calming atmosphere, like taking a walk in the woods during your office break.

Award: BLOOM has been chosen amongst the winning projects by the official comitee of Lecce & EduVita organizations, in the south of Italy.
